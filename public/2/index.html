W/ Phil Moorhouse - Jeremy Corbyn Suspended, Brexit "Gets Real" and Fighting Political Fatigue

Posted on Friday, Oct 30, 2020
In this episode, we chat with Phil Moorhouse, political commentator with his YouTube Channel “A Different Bias”. We talk about independent media, political fatigue and Brexit. We also discuss Jeremy Corbyn’s suspension and what this does to Labour’s recent positive momentum.
